Hi, I'm Mohammadreza Narimani!

Ph.D. Candidate at the University of California, Davis

I am a research assistant at the Digital Agriculture Laboratory, focusing on remote sensing in agriculture. My work spans from leaf-level analysis using handheld hyperspectral devices to large-scale agricultural monitoring employing drone-based RGB, multispectral, hyperspectral, and LiDAR sensors, as well as satellite imagery. I utilize machine learning and deep learning models to extract insights from these diverse data sources.
