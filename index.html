Hi, I'm Abiodun Emmanuel Abioye!

Engineering and Computer Science Instructor at the Reedley College, Reedley, California

I am a Engineering and Computer Science Instructor at the Reedley College, Reedley, and a Precision Agriculture Researcher at the Digital Agriculture Laboratory. My research focuses on robotics in agriculture, proximal sensing in agriculture, ranging from leaf-level analysis using handheld hyperspectral devices to large-scale agricultural monitoring with drone-based RGB, multispectral, hyperspectral, and LiDAR sensors. I also work with sensors and IoT systems deployed in farms to collect real-time data. I utilize machine learning and deep learning models to extract insights from these diverse data sources.
