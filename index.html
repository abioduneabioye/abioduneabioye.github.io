Hi, I'm Mohammadreza Narimani!

Ph.D. Candidate at the University of California, Davis

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of California, Davis, and a research assistant at the Digital Agriculture Laboratory. My research focuses on remote sensing in agriculture, ranging from leaf-level analysis using handheld hyperspectral devices to large-scale agricultural monitoring with drone-based RGB, multispectral, hyperspectral, and LiDAR sensors, as well as satellite imagery. I also work with sensors and IoT systems deployed in farms to collect real-time data. I utilize machine learning and deep learning models to extract insights from these diverse data sources.
