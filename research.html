Sentinel2 Vegetation Index Visualizer

Digital Agriculture Laboratory, UC Davis

A Google Earth Engine (GEE) script that facilitates dynamic visualization and analysis of multiple vegetation indices using Sentinel-2 imagery for user-defined regions and time periods. This tool enables researchers and agricultural professionals to monitor crop health and environmental conditions with precision.

Interactive Mapping
